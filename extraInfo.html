Bear Bear is currently six years old. He was designed and created by the Frence brand, Moulin Roty. I think he's very unique because he has a zipper on his right side (which you can see in the photos section) about six inches long. Once you open it, his pocket is about nine inches long. I got Bear Bear on my fourth birthday, when my dad came back from China. This website is made for Bear Bear, and I hope you enjoy looking at it as much as I i did making it.
