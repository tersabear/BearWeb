More Bear Bear Info



Bear Bear is a stuffed animal bear designed and created by the Frence brand, Moulin Roty. Bear Bear has green feet and an orange nose, as you can see. He weighs 0.8 pounds. On his right side, he has a zipper, which can hold items. If you want to see more photos of Bear Bear, go to the photos section

I got Bear Bear on my fourth birthday. My dad had just come back from China and he brought with him a stuffed animal, which we later named Bear Bear. Bear Bear is currently six, although I'm not sure if he still will be by the time you see this.
